**Stockly GTM Research**


Research Methodology
Started with industries showing complex inventory needs—automotive parts, industrial distribution, electronics, and manufacturing. Used web research and AI tools to find US companies with $50M–$2B revenue and multi-warehouse operations. LinkedIn searches identified decision makers like VP Supply Chain and Operations Directors. Structured everything in Excel using pandas for clean 20-company target list + Netstock competitor analysis.

AI Tools Used
Perplexity for company discovery and market signals, LinkedIn-style queries for buyer identification, Excel for GTM data organization. Manual checks confirmed company fit and supply chain complexity for all targets.

Why These 20 Companies
Perfect ICP match: mid-market scale ($50M–$2B revenue), 200-5000 employees, multi-warehouse ops, high SKU complexity. Replaced enterprise giants with true mid-market leaders like Motion Industries ($1.5B MRO, 600+ branches), Sonepar USA ($1.2B electrical, 400+ locations), Winsupply ($1B+ plumbing/HVAC). All face branch imbalances, MRO volatility, DC throughput challenges.

Key GTM Insights

Mid-market gap - Netstock owns SMBs (<$500M), leaving $50M-$2B distributors hungry for advanced AI

Inventory Risk Reports convert - Personalized benchmarks showing "$XM excess risk" grab VP attention immediately

Motion Industries = top target - Massive MRO complexity + active supply chain hiring = perfect Stockly fit

ERP = buying trigger - All targets run NetSuite/SAP/Dynamics = instant integration advantage

Campaign Concept
Inventory Risk Intelligence Report
Personalized report using industry benchmarks to estimate excess inventory risk, stockout exposure, and working capital opportunity. Designed to drive high engagement from supply chain leaders via gated landing pages.
